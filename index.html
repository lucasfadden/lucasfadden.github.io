About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master’s by Research (MScR) in Computer Science at the University of Bristol, affiliated with the Bristol Interaction Group (BIG) and the Cabot Institute for the Environment. My research explores how human-computer interaction (HCI) can contribute to sustainable technologies, particularly through the study of user interaction with energy-efficient systems like heat pumps.

I'm especially interested in the potential for AI to support sustainable infrastructure by bridging technological design and human behavior.
